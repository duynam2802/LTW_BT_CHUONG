Apple Bavarian Torte (★ ★ ★ ★)

A classic European torte baked in a springform pan. Cream cheese, scliced almonds, and apples make this the perfect holiday treat (12 servings).

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

INGREDIENTS

  1. Preheat oven to F (230 C)
  2. Cream together butter, sugar, and flour
  3. Press crust mixture into the flat bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. Set aside
  4. In a medium bowl, blend cream cheese and sugar. Beat in egg and pour cheese mixture over crust
  5. Mix apples with sugar and cinnamon. Spread apple mixture over
  6. Bake for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) and continue baking for 25 minutes
  7. Sprinkle top with sliced almonds. Lightly cool pan
